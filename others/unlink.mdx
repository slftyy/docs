description: 'How to unlink your Rockstar Games from Epic Games.'
icon: 'stars'
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Go to the [Rockstar Games Website](https://support.rockstargames.com) and sign in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
    Go to the [Support Category](https://support.rockstargames.com/categories/200013106?step=44cd3a8b).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Third Step">
    Now, put your `Epic Account ID` in the first field. You can find it [here](https://www.epicgames.com/account/personal).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fourth Step">
    Copy and Paste this in the description and put in all your details.

    ```javascript Copy and Paste
    I would like to unlink my Rockstar account from Epic.

    Epic Details
    Name:
    ID:

    Rockstar Details
    Email:
    Name:

    I understand that this process will result in the loss of all my GTA Online progress, and I am okay with that.
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fourth Step">
    Send it.
  </Step>
</Steps>
