## Receiving 
    
<Accordion title="Method 1 (Safest)">
  We will invite you to a Family and you can withdraw the Money from the Family account.
</Accordion>

    
<Accordion title="Method 2">
  We will give you the Money over a Trade in exchange of some clothes/items.
</Accordion>


<Accordion title="Method 3">
  We will just give you the Money over hand.
</Accordion>
