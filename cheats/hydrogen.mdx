## Redeeming

<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    After purchasing and receiving the key, go to the **[Hydrogen Website](https://hydrogen.ac/auth/register)** and create an account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
    Now, login to your created account and press the button `Redeem Key` in the top right corner, paste the license key into the field to redeem it, it will bind it to your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Injecting

<Info>
  Make sure to disable your antivirus before injecting the cheat.
</Info>


<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Start your Game and stay in the Server list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
    Run the Cheat as an administrator.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Third Step">
    Log in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fourth Step">
    Select your Game and press Inject.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fifth Step">
    Connect to a Server and have fun.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  After injecting the cheat, you can use the cheat menu by pressing the `INSERT` key.
